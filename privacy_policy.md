# Privacy Policy

**Effective Date:** June 2026

## 1. Information Collection
The **Games!!** Discord bot ("the Bot") only collects the minimum amount of data required to function. 
- **Server IDs and Channel IDs:** The Bot stores the Discord Server ID and configured Channel ID solely to route automated release notifications to the correct channel.
- **Message Content:** The Bot temporarily reads message content to process commands (e.g., `!search`, `!ping`). The Bot does **not** store or log any message content, user messages, or search queries permanently.
- **User IDs:** The Bot does not store Discord User IDs, except for evaluating the permissions of the designated Bot Owner.

## 2. Information Usage
The collected data (Guild IDs and Channel IDs) is stored locally on the hosting machine in a `database/` directory. It is used strictly for internal routing and operational functionality. 
We do not sell, rent, or share any data with third parties.

## 3. Independent Operation
The Bot acts merely as a web-scraping and RSS-reading utility. The developer (Sharon) and this Bot have **no affiliation, sponsorship, partnership, or connection whatsoever** with `online-fix.me` or any of its administrators. 

## 4. Data Deletion
If you kick the Bot from your Discord server, it will automatically purge your server's configured Channel ID from its local database during its routine cleanup cycle. You may also contact the Bot Owner to manually delete your server's configuration.
